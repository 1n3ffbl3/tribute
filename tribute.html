Tracy Chevalier

Author of famous "Girl with a Pearl Earring"

Tracy Chevalier with tree, by Jon Drori, CC BY-SA 3.0

On her website we can read that she was born in October 1962 and grew up in Washington, DC, moving to England after graduating with a BA in English from Oberlin College (Ohio). For several years she worked as a reference book editor, while writing short stories in my spare time. In 1993 she quit to do an MA in Creative Writing from the University of East Anglia (Norwich, England). Afterwards she juggled freelance editing with writing until eventually she was able to write full-time.



Tarcy Chevalier is the author of following novels:

  • The Virgin Blue (1997)
  • Girl With a Pearl Earring (1999)
  • Falling Angels (2001)
  • The Lady and the Unicorn (2003)
  • Burning Bright (2007)
  • Remarkable Creatures (2009)
  • The Last Runaway (2013)
  • Reader, I Married Him (2016)
  • At the Edge of the Orchard (2016)
  • New Boy (2017)

"But what is going on when my pen is scratching, pausing, scratching? I am performing a magic trick that still surprises me. I am in this world, at my desk or kitchen table, but I am simultaneously holding in my head another world full of people I have never physically met but know to their core. That world and those people pour out through my pen: rough – often very rough – but insistent."

Tracy Chevalier, My writing day in "The Guardian"
